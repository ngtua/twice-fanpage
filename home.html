TWICE IS LIFE

Background

Twice (Korean: 트와이스; RR: Teuwaiseu; Japanese: トゥワイス, Hepburn: Tuwaisu; commonly stylized as TWICE) is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed under the television program Sixteen (2015) and debuted on October 20, 2015, with the extended play (EP) The Story Begins.

Why I love TWICE

When I was a college freshman I became extremely stressed over several things in my life: choosing a career path, socializing, self-confidence and loving myself. At this time, TWICE released the song YES or YES. Although I had been listening to K-Pop music for some time already and was an arguably larger fan of boy groups like NCT, something about this particular song helped me feel better, gave me just a tiny bit of joy in my life. Since then, I have become a follower of TWICE, a Once. Please enjoy the photo above of TWICE performing for the 2022 Twice 4th World Tour "III" at the Dickies Arena in Fort Worth, TX.
